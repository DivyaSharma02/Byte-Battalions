What is Upstyling?

Upcycling is the process of transforming donated clothes into fashionable items. Our team performs a quality check on the donated clothes, and if they pass, we collaborate with stylists to create trendy pieces from the fabric.

How It Works

  1. Quality Check: We examine the donated clothes to ensure they are in good condition.
  2. Collaboration: We partner with fashion stylists to upstyle the fabric.
  3. Fashionable Items: The fabric is transformed into trendy, fashionable items.

FAQs

What types of clothes can be upcycled?

We accept a wide range of clothing items, as long as they are in good condition and made of upcyclable fabric.

How long does the upcycling process take?

The duration varies depending on the complexity of the upcycling project and stylist availability.

Can I purchase upcycled items?

Yes, upcycled items are available for purchase on our platform. Check out our Upcycled Collection section.
