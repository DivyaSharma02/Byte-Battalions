What is Recycling?

Recycling involves giving donated clothes to organizations that specialize in recycling fabrics. This helps reduce waste and promotes sustainable practices.

How It Works

  1. Collection: We collect donated clothes from customers.
  2. Partnership: We partner with recycling organizations.
  3. Recycling: The clothes are recycled into new fabrics and materials.

FAQs

What types of clothes can be recycled?

We accept all types of clothing items, regardless of their condition.

What happens to the recycled materials?

The recycled materials are used to create new fabrics and other products, promoting a circular economy.

How can I contribute to recycling?

You can donate your old clothes at our designated collection points or through our pick-up service.
